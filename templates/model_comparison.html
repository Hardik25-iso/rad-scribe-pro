Why BioGPT? (Model B)
Transitioning from LSTM to a pre-trained BioGPT decoder allowed the model to understand medical terminology natively, treating complex words like "cardiomegaly" as single tokens.
Counterfactual RAG (Model D)
Instead of just retrieving similar images, Model D retrieves the top-3 similar AND top-2 dissimilar cases. This forces the model to learn the distinct differences between normal and abnormal scans.
CheXBert Verification (Model E)
To ensure clinical safety, Model E uses a separate disease classifier. Findings generated by the LLM are cross-referenced with the classifier, achieving a 78.25 MicroF1 score.
